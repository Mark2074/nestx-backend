8️⃣ MODERATION & SAFETY 8.1 Content Monitoring NestX monitors content and user activity to maintain a safe environment and ensure compliance with platform rules. 8.2 Human Moderation Final moderation decisions are made by NestX administrators. Depending on the case, content may be restored, removed, or subject to further action. 8.3 Reporting System Users can report content or behavior that violates platform rules. Reported content may be temporarily hidden while under review.
