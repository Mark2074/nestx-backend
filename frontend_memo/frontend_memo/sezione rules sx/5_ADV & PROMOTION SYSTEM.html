ADV & PROMOTION SYSTEM 5.1 Internal ADV What They Are Internal ADV are promotional contents created within NestX to increase the visibility of a profile, an event, or an initiative. They are not external advertisements and are part of the platform ecosystem. Who Can Create Them Internal ADV can be created only by creators. Duration Each ADV has a limited and predefined duration. Once expired, the ADV is automatically removed. Limits Maximum number of active ADV per user Limited publication frequency Visibility is not guaranteed and is managed by the platform through rotation and priority rules 5.2 Moderation of ADV Review All ADV are subject to review before publication. Approval An ADV is approved if it complies with platform rules, Terms, and content standards. Rejection An ADV may be rejected if it: Violates platform rules or limits Is misleading, spam, or not coherent with the creator profile Contains prohibited content Rejected ADV are not published. 5.3 Difference Between ADV & Posts What’s the Difference Posts are standard social contents published in the feed ADV are promotional contents with enhanced visibility and limited duration Why They Exist ADV are designed to: Increase visibility for profiles or events Keep the feed clean and non-intrusive Clearly separate promotional content from social content
