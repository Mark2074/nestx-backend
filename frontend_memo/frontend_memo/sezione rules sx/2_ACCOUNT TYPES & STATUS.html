2️⃣ ACCOUNT TYPES & STATUS On NestX there are different account types and statuses. Each level enables specific features and has defined limits, designed to ensure security, authenticity, and sustainability for both users and the platform. 2.1 Base User What a Base User can do Register and create a profile. Publish posts in the feed, in compliance with platform rules. View public content. Follow other users. Like content. Access the main sections of the platform. View public events and live sessions (view-only). Send a limited number of messages per day in chats. Purchase tokens (when enabled). What a Base User cannot do Monetize content or events in any form. Redeem tokens. Access Creator-only features. Use advanced search filters. Chat without limitations. 📌 Note Any user may create events or live sessions, but only approved Creators can monetize and redeem tokens. 2.2 Verified User What verification is Verification is a procedure to confirm the authenticity of a profile, carried out through a verification video according to the methods specified by the platform. Verification: does not require showing the face ensures that the profile represents a real person increases trust and credibility within the community Benefits Ability to interact freely in live chats. Increased profile credibility. Full access to standard social features. Limits Verification does not enable monetization by itself. It does not replace VIP status. It does not grant Creator privileges. 2.3 VIP Status What VIP is VIP is an additional status, not an account type. It can be activated by Base Users, Verified Users, and Creators. Concrete benefits Advanced search filters (profile type, language, geographic area). Higher limits and greater freedom in chats. More personalized feeds. Access to additional content information. Priority access to certain platform features. What VIP is not It is not a Creator account. It does not enable direct monetization. It does not guarantee visibility or earnings. It is not an external promotion tool. Limits VIP benefits are active only while the status is valid. When VIP expires, all related benefits are automatically removed. VIP does not bypass moderation rules or legal requirements. 2.4 Creator Account Requirements Verified identity. Acceptance of Creator-specific terms and conditions. Compliance with applicable legal and fiscal regulations. Full adherence to the platform’s content rules. Rights Create and manage events and live sessions. Receive tokens from users. Monetize tokens according to platform rules. Promote events and content within allowed limits. Access dedicated Creator tools. Responsibilities Publish only compliant content. Respect users and the community. Avoid misleading or manipulative behavior. Properly manage live sessions and events. Accept platform reviews and compliance checks. Allowed monetization Monetization is allowed only through official platform tokens. External or unauthorized monetization methods are not permitted. Tokens received are convertible and redeemable only if the Creator is approved. The platform reserves the right to suspend monetization in case of violations. 📌 General note NestX is built on a balance between freedom of expression and clear rules. Any misuse of platform features, regardless of account type or status, may result in limitations, suspensions, or revocation of privileges.
