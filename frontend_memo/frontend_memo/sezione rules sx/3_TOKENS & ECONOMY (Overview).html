3️⃣ TOKENS & ECONOMY 3.1 What Tokens Are Tokens are the internal currency of NestX. Within the platform, Tokens replace real currency and are used to perform all interactions and purchases supported by the system. NestX uses Tokens to: simplify transactions avoid direct payments between users ensure a consistent and controlled experience 3.2 How Tokens Are Used Tokens can be used only within NestX for: Chat sending messages in Live or in contexts where Tokens are required interacting with creators during live sessions Live supporting a Live unlocking interactive features Events purchasing event tickets accessing event-related content or features ADV promoting events, Lives, or content using internal visibility services Other uses Tokens are used for all internal purchases and platform features, present or future. 3.3 Monetization Rules Who can monetize Only Creator accounts can redeem Tokens and convert them into currency. Who cannot monetize All other accounts can use Tokens only within the platform. 3.4 What Tokens Are NOT Tokens cannot be used outside of NestX.
