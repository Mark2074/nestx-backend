6️⃣ SHOWCASE 6.1 What Is the Showcase What It Is The Showcase is a dedicated area of NestX for displaying real products created by the user, such as handmade items, crafts, personal creations, or original projects. Purpose It allows VIP users to present their creations to a wider audience in a space separate from the social feed, designed to highlight tangible content without mixing it with posts or advertising. 6.2 Who Can Access It Requirements Access is reserved exclusively for VIP users The account must be active and compliant with NestX Terms Limitations The Showcase is not self-service Publication is available only by platform proposal or approval No access for Base users or non-VIP Creators 6.3 Benefits Visibility Showcase content receives a dedicated and prominent placement Higher visibility compared to standard organic content Difference from Ads (ADV) The Showcase is not direct advertising Content must be real, authentic, and created by the user Misleading, aggressive, or purely promotional content is not allowed 6.4 Moderation & Control Review Process All Showcase content is subject to review The platform may reject or remove content at any time Content Consistency Content must be consistent with: the user’s profile the style and values of NestX Misleading or non-authentic content is not permitted
