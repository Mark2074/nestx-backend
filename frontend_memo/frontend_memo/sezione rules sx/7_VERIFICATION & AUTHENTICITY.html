7️⃣ VERIFICATION & AUTHENTICITY Profile Verification What it is Profile verification is intended only to confirm the authenticity of the declared profile (single or couple). How it works The user must submit a short verification video (a few seconds) showing: The silhouette of the declared profile 1 person → single profile 2 people → couple profile The face is not required and may be hidden, masked, or not visible A handwritten note clearly showing: username date the word “NestX” The verification video is publicly accessible via a link on the profile. What it guarantees That the profile represents real people That the declared profile type is authentic That the profile is not fake or automated Profile verification does not apply to published content.
